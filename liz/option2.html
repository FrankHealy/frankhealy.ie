About

From nursing to therapy—a journey of transformation and healing

My Background

From Nursing to Therapy

After dedicating years to nursing, witnessing the profound connection between physical and mental wellbeing, I felt called to deepen my impact through therapeutic counselling.

My nursing background provides invaluable insight into holistic health, understanding that true healing encompasses mind, body, and spirit.

My Approach

Evidence-Based Compassion

I integrate evidence-based therapeutic approaches with genuine compassion. Every person carries within them the capacity for growth and transformation.

My practice combines Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, trauma-informed care, and solution-focused approaches, tailored specifically to your unique needs.

"Life's second chapters can be your most meaningful—I'm living proof"

My journey from nursing to therapy wasn't just a career change—it was a complete transformation. After decades of caring for the body, I discovered my true calling in healing the soul. This transition taught me that it's never too late to begin again, to find new purpose, to write a different story.

I honor the inherent resilience within every person. Therapy isn't about fixing what's broken—it's about understanding your story, recognizing your strengths, and supporting you as you discover your own path forward. Your second chapter is waiting.
